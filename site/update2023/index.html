About the event
Join us for the annual Update event, where top companies in the field of computer science come together to meet, interview, and engage with students at our university. This is a unique opportunity to get a glimpse into the industry and learn from experts in the field. Attendees will have the chance to participate in brief interviews, workshops, and talks led by professionals in the industry. You will have the opportunity to network and make valuable connections, as well as expand your knowledge in the field. Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity to showcase your skills, learn from the best, and kickstart your career in computer science. Register today and take the first step towards a brighter future! We look forward to seeing you at Update!
