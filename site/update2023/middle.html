The General Pass grants you the opportunity to attend the Update 2023 event, which includes participating in workshops, hearing talks, and enjoying a good time with your friends and with the FCUP community. With this pass, you will have access to all the activities and events scheduled for the duration of the event. You will be able to learn new skills, meet new people, and have fun. On the other hand, the Hackton Pass will grant you the opportunity to be challenged by a problem that will be announced at the moment the event begins. This challenge will be formulated and assessed by the Critical Tech Works company. The Hackton Pass is a good experience to share your skills and evaluate them in a real-world problem-solving scenario. You will have the opportunity to work with other like-minded individuals and to learn from experienced professionals in the field. Whether you choose the General Pass or the Hackton Pass, you will have a great time at the Update 2023 event. We look forward to seeing you there!
